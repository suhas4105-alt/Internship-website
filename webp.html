Internship Resource & Execution Plan

An interactive guide to the 30-day research program.

This internship is a 30-day intensive program focused on translating cutting-edge theory into practice across three core domains: Robotics, Smart Systems, and Power Electronics. This overview provides a high-level look at the program's structure and the distribution of projects.

The program features 11 distinct projects, categorized into three main research tracks. This chart shows the focus of the internship by project count.

Each research track has a specific set of required components. Explore the Bill of Materials for each domain below to see the hardware and software involved. Click on any track to expand its component list.

Hardware:

  • Sensors: MyoWare Muscle Sensors (v2.0) or AD8232, Ag/AgCl Electrodes, FSR (Force Sensitive Resistors).
  • Controller: ESP32 Dev Module or Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense (for onboard ML).
  • Actuators: Micro Air Pumps (12V/5V), Solenoid Valves (3-way), Servo Motors.
  • Materials: Ecoflex 00-30/00-50, PLA/PETG Filament, 3D Printed Bellows (TPU), Metal Bellows samples.
  • Power: 12V DC Power Supply, LiPo Batteries (7.4V or 11.1V).

Software/Tools:

  • Python (TensorFlow/PyTorch/Scikit-Learn).
  • SolidWorks/Fusion 360 (CAD).
  • MATLAB/Simulink (Signal processing toolbox).

Project: Solar Integrated Hydroponics

  • Structure: PVC Pipes (NFT channels), Reservoir Tank (20L), Submersible Pump.
  • Solar: 50W-100W Solar Panel, Charge Controller, Lead Acid/LiFePO4 Battery.
  • Intelligence: Raspberry Pi 4, Pi Camera Module v2.
  • Sensors: pH Sensor, EC Sensor, DHT11, Water Level Float Switch.

Project: P2P Energy Trading & Blockchain

  • Compute Nodes: 3x Raspberry Pi (representing prosumers/consumers).
  • Metering: PZEM-004T AC Energy Meters, Current Transformers (CT).
  • Switching: 4-Channel Relay Module (to simulate power transfer).
  • Network: Local Wi-Fi Router (isolated network).
  • Software: Ethereum (Geth) or Hyperledger Fabric, Node.js, Web3.js.

Common Components for All Converters/Inverters:

  • Controller: Wavect Controller (FPGA/SoC based).
  • Switches: SiC MOSFETs or IGBT modules (rated 600V+).
  • Drivers: Isolated Gate Drivers (e.g., TLP250, SKYPER).
  • Passives: Power Inductors, DC Link Capacitors, Current Sensors (LEM LA-55P), Voltage Sensors (LV-25P).
  • Simulation: MATLAB/Simulink with HDL Coder support.
  • Test Gear: Digital Oscilloscope (4-channel), Differential Probes, DC Programmable Power Supply, Rheostat (Load).
